5 Tips for a Beautiful Lawn in London, ON

Maintaining a beautiful lawn in London, Ontario requires understanding our unique climate and soil conditions. Here are five essential tips to keep your grass healthy and vibrant throughout the growing season.

1. Water deeply but less frequently: London's clay soil retains moisture well, so water your lawn 2-3 times per week with deep soaking rather than daily light watering. 2. Fertilize in spring and fall: Apply a balanced fertilizer in early spring and late fall to promote healthy growth. 3. Mow at the right height: Keep grass at 2.5-3 inches to promote deep root growth and drought resistance.

4. Overseed in early fall: September is the perfect time in London to overseed thin areas for a thicker lawn next spring. 5. Control weeds early: Apply pre-emergent herbicide in early spring to prevent crabgrass and other weeds from taking hold. Following these tips will ensure your London lawn stays green and healthy all season long.
